Product Solution
EcoMap — Smart Urban Planning Tool
🗺️

Heat Zone Mapping

Pinpoint high-temperature areas across the city with color-coded risk overlays. Decision-makers see the full picture instantly — no spreadsheet needed.

🌿

Green Priority Score

A composite index ranking zones by intervention urgency, combining temperature, green cover deficit, and population density into a single actionable score.

📍

Smart Recommendations

Zone-level suggestions: tree planting locations, park development zones, and cooling corridor routes — optimized for maximum urban temperature impact.

📊

Authority Dashboard

Real-time decision support built for NDMC and MCD planners. Track progress, compare zones, and measure the impact of green interventions over time.

Primary users: NDMC, MCD, Urban development authorities  ·  Secondary users: Environmental NGOs, Urban researchers
